Instructions

  • Start by mixing water and salt in a bowl, dissolving the salt in the water.
  • Add about 10% of the total flour weight to the water and stir to combine.
  • Dissolve the yeast in the water mixture.
  • Let the dough rest for about 10 minutes to activate the yeast.
  • Start the dough mixer on low speed and gradually add the remaining flour.
  • Knead the dough for about 20 minutes on low speed until a smooth, firm dough forms.
  • Cover the dough with a damp cloth and let it rest for about 2 hours at room temperature (around 25°C).
  • After 2 hours, divide the dough into 180-250 gram portions and shape them into balls by hand.
  • Allow the dough balls to rise for 4-6 hours in a covered container at room temperature.
  • Once the dough has risen, lightly flour a work surface and press the dough ball into a round shape by pressing from the center outwards, creating a raised edge (corricione) around the pizza.
  • Preheat your oven or pizza oven to a temperature of around 430°C at the base and 485°C at the top.
  • Top the pizza with a thin layer of tomato sauce, followed by mozzarella and other toppings of your choice.
  • Place the pizza in the oven and bake for 60-90 seconds, depending on the temperature and your oven.
